Optimizing Diagnostic Accuracy of Clinical Red Flags in RASopathies.
Bobbio E, Caiazza M, Monda E, et al. — American journal of medical genetics. Part A 2026
  • Among 669 patients, 34 were clinically suspected of RASopathy, with a confirmed diagnosis in 24 cases (71%).
  • ML analysis identified PVS and facial dysmorphisms as the top predictors.
  • While the ML model showed high performance in this derivation cohort, external validation is needed to confirm its generalizability.

Influence of Technical Performance Score on Outcomes After Tetralogy of Fallot Repair in a Low-Middle-Income Country.
Miana L, Nathan M, Manuel V, et al. — World journal for pediatric & congenital heart surgery 2026
  • Cardiopulmonary bypass time, ECMO, and DSC were associated with major complications.
  • Technical Performance Score class 3 correlated with longer LOS, but multivariable analysis identified low weight and ECMO as independent LOS predictors.
  • Surgical quality remains essential for better outcomes.

Genetic Investigation of Corrected QT Interval Sensitivity to Oral Bepridil Hydrochloride Hydrate in Patients With Atrial Fibrillation.
Ishibashi N, Furutani M, Nakashima M, et al. — Journal of the American Heart Association 2026
  • After quality control, 443 patients were analyzed in the genome-wide association study.
  • Of these, 122 (28%) exhibited QTc prolongation.
  • Under the recessive model, rs12622919, an intronic variant in the FSHR (follicle-stimulating hormone receptor) gene, showed a suggestive association.

Genetic etiology and phenotypic characteristics of fetuses with 11q deletion syndrome (11q23.3-q25).
Zhang T, Cong X, Li Z, et al. — Experimental and therapeutic medicine 2026
  • CMA identified the deletions while karyotyping revealed underlying structural rearrangements, including balanced translocations in both cases.
  • Prenatal ultrasound identified cardiac anomalies, such as ventricular septal defects and truncus arteriosus.
  • The results demonstrated that the phenotypic expression of 11q- syndrome varies according to the size and gene content of the deleted region.
